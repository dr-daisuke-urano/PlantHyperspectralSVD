Sample spectra obtained from the hyperspectral images (which can be found in the SPECIM_sample_images directory). 
The CSV files contain the following information: ImageID, PlantID, label (treatment), leaf area, radius, and reflectance spectra from whole, central, paracentral, and peripheral regions.
Marchantia polymorpha plants were grown under various conditions: control (no stress), phosphate deficiency, nitrate deficiency, and iron deficiency. For more details, please refer to Krishnamoorthi S (2024) Cell Reports and Krishnamoorthi S (2024) Figshare.

IDs and corresponding conditions:

323: Nitrate deficiency
347: Iron deficiency
397: Phosphate deficiency
